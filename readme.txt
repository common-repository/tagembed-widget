== How to Embed Instagram feed on WordPress? ==

* Click on "Add Feed" & select "Instagram" or "Instagram Business" to collect Instagram feeds.
* Choose your Instagram feed filter connection type, such as Instagram handle, hashtag, mention, profile, stories, Reels, IGTV, etc., to fetch Instagram feeds according to your preference.
* Select a pre-built theme (like photos, gallery). Personalize the Instagram feed style & filter settings
* Click on the Display tab Section to preview the Instagram Feed & Copy ShortCode to [Embed Instagram Feed on WordPress](https://tagembed.com/blog/embed-instagram-feed-on-wordpress/).
* Paste the shortcode on your desired page or post & update it to [embed Instagram feed](https://tagembed.com/blog/embed-instagram-feed-on-website-for-free/) Widget successfully.

 == Installation ==

Installation is free, quick, and easy. Set up Tagembed in minutes.

**Method 1: From the Plugin directory**

* Go to the Plugins page in your WordPress site’s dashboard.
* Click on the “Add New” button.
* Search for “Tagembed”.
* Click on the “Install” button next to it, then click “Activate”.
* Choose the network you want to collect content from example – Instagram Feed
* Customize the Widget using different theme and layout options
* Copy the short code and paste it where you want to display Instagram widget

**Method 2: Upload the Zip File**

* Download the Tagembed Zip file.
 * Upload the file in the /wp-content/plugins directory of your WordPress website.
* Activate the plugin.
* Choose the network (Eg:“Google Reviews”) and get started.

**Step by Step guide to Add Instagram Feed on WordPress Website:**

* Select Instagram as your network.
* Now choose your Instagram Feed type from Instagram Handle, Instagram Hashtag, Instagram Stories, Instagram Reels, Instagram Mentions and click on “Create Feed”.
* Now authorize your Instagram account to collect posts from Instagram.
* Your Instagram feed will be now collected to Tagembed plugin.
* You can now proceed to customize Instagram Feed by using different themes & layout options. Then choose relevant Instagram Feed from moderation panel so that you don't showcase irrelevant Instagram feed.
* Finally, go to the embed tab & copy the Instagram feed WordPress embed code.
* Paste the embed code in the backend of the page or posts you would like to display the Instagram Feed in.
* Save the changes made & you're done.
* Your Instagram Feed is now live on your website.
